Monday — Story (IG + Facebook)
Office Hours & Location
Every Monday we publish a branded Story featuring the firm's office hours, address, and a direct call to action. The goal is to stay top of mind with former clients, referral sources, and prospective clients. Copy rotates each week to keep the content fresh and avoid repetition.
Tuesday — Carousel (IG + Facebook)
Case Results & Client Education
4 to 6-slide carousels with bold typography, dark backgrounds, and direct copy. Topics rotate between real case outcomes, personal injury education, and firm differentiators. The format drives saves and shares, and the consistent visual style reinforces the firm's polished, professional identity.
Wednesday — Review (IG Story + Facebook)
Google My Business Testimonial
A real GMB review is turned into a branded Story graphic. Authentic client testimonials build immediate trust with prospective clients and reinforce credibility with referring attorneys and contacts. This content pillar requires no copywriting — the client's voice does the work.
Friday — Myth Busting (IG + Facebook)
Personal Injury Myths
Every Friday we debunk a common misconception about personal injury law. This format educates the audience, generates organic shares, and positions Flanagan Law as the firm that speaks plainly and honestly. It also supports the SEO strategy by targeting question-based search queries.
Sunday — Video UGC (IG Reel + Facebook)
Brendan On Camera
The attorney speaks directly to camera, explaining a case, a legal concept, or a recent development such as Georgia's SB 68. UGC-style video consistently outperforms designed graphics in organic reach on both Instagram and Facebook. It humanizes the firm and builds the kind of trust no graphic can replace.
